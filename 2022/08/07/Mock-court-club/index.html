Mock court club Summary

发表于 2022-08-07 | 分类于 Summary

##My experience in Senior 1

####My Club：Mock Court Club

####Time：First year of high school

Position as a witness

Frequency of activities once a week

I joined the club by chance since once I happened to see a schoolmate’s game.I was curious to see how the process would play out in the courtroom, and I wonder if I could handle the challenge and adapt to it by using my testimony fluently.

My job was to understand my part of the evidence thoroughly and summaries it in an 800-word summary, eventually performing it by heart and answering the questions prepared by the opposing counsel.

I had many amazing experiences throughout the proceedings. One time, a lawyer asked me a sensitive question about suspicion of a crime, and I was able to point it out to the judge and dismiss it, saving my own lawyer time and disrupting the other lawyer’s rhythm.This technique was taught by my senior,I was excited to use it in practise. It has also sharpened my ability to respond to different situations in spoken English.

This has given me a lot to gain. I can see from the judge’s performance that eye contact and a smile can add credibility, and I realize that it is important to be respectful in all aspects when interacting with people and not be shy. In the future, I will have to be able to deal with the situation calmly, just as I will be able to deal with the opposing counsel’s difficulties without fear. In future competitions, I would like to learn more about the characters through the way they speak. In this competition, the old man I performed did not seem realistic, so I can summarize the character traits of the character and combine them with my daily life to come up with a more relevant way of acting.It made me realize the importance of being well prepared and meticulous in what you do.
